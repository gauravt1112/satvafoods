*Milk and milk products used are from dairy farms following organic practices.
All our ingredients and inputs are procured from farms and vendors who have passed the required Certifications by the testing centers accredited by the AGRICULTURAL AND PROCESSED FOOD PRODUCTS EXPORT DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (APEDA) of India for following NPOP Standards

When we call a food ingredient Organic, it means that there is minimal use of off-farm inputs and the practices used are to restore, maintain, and enhance ecological harmony. There are no synthetic pesticides or fertilizers and petroleum based fertilizers used during the production of the food product. The processing takes place in the absence of irradiation, industrial solvents or chemical food additives.
