FOUNDER

Dipal Shah

Satva Foods is a started by DIPAL SHAH, a C.A by profession and a successful homemaker, who have always given utmost importance to the health of the family, and researched a lot on organic food. This experience made her realise the amount of chemicals/ pesticides/ fertilizers used during the production and processing of the fruits, vegetables which ultimately reduce the nutritional value of the foods being consumed.

This motivated her to start using few organic wellness products for her children and on seeing the change in them and their health in a span of 1 month, she decided to extent her knowledge and let it benefit more people, which gave birth to Satva Foods.

Believing in the motto of right to good health, she chose the path to be a catalyst in improving the health of people to the extent possible by providing food cooked with all ingredients being APEDA certified as organic.

ADVISING NUTRITIONIST

Pallavi Srivastva

Having an experience of over 5 yrs and a long list of satisfied clientele from all sections of the society, she is a Post Graduate in Nutrition and has specialised in Clinical Nutrition and Cardiac Nutrition, Pallavi’s passion for Nutrition and Wellness inspired her to launch her own fitness studio ‘Q-SLIM’(www.pallavisqslimfitness.com).

Her articles continue to be a part of various recognized newspapers like Bombay Times, Afternoon DC, Radio channels like big 92.7FM and websites like Yahoo.com, Healthmeup.com, etc.

Our Nutritionist clinches that the food picked up is healthy to make your days meal enticing to keep you on foot amidst those long working hours.
