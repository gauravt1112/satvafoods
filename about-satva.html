What is Satva Foods?

The core principle of Satva team is to ensure the deliverance of the best quality of organic food at an affordable price.

Satva foods is designed to give good and healthier food not only for your body but also for your mind.

Dipal Shah started Satva foods to bring alive her vision of providing healthier food cooked from 100% organic ingredients right from salt to pulses.

We believe our customers to be a part of our family. We understand our growth lies with their wellbeing, so utmost care is been taken at all levels right from sourcing food ingredients, to its preparation, packing, etc., leaving no scope of compromise at any levels.
